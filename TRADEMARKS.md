# Trademark Notice

CUA-Gym-Hub is a research project: a suite of mock web applications used to
train and evaluate computer-use AI agents under controlled, reproducible
conditions. None of the mocks in this repository are real products. They
do not connect to the services of any third party, do not transmit data
to any third party, and are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored
by any trademark holder.

## Naming

To reduce any risk of confusion with real-world products, every mock
displays a deliberately altered brand name in its UI — e.g., a session
labelled `gmail_mock` renders as "Xmail", a session labelled `slack_mock`
renders as "Xlack", and so on. Directory names retain the original
identifiers only for engineering reference (`gmail_mock` is shorter and
clearer than `xmail_email_client_mock`); they are not surfaced to users
of the running mock.
